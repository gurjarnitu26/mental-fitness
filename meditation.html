Meditation Tips for Beginners

Find Your Space

Choose a quiet, comfortable place where you won't be disturbed. It doesn't have to be perfect - just somewhere you can relax.

Start Small

Begin with just 5 minutes a day. It's better to meditate for a short time consistently than to try long sessions occasionally.

Be Kind to Yourself

Don't worry if your mind wanders - that's normal! Gently bring your attention back to your breath or chosen focus.

Consistency Matters

Try to meditate at the same time each day. Morning or evening routines work well for many people.
